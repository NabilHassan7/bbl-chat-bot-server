Q: What are some ethical considerations for developing AI?
A: Transparency, fairness, accountability, and privacy are key.
   Developers should also consider long-term societal impacts.

Q: How can we ensure AI systems are unbiased?
A: Diverse training data, regular audits, and clear documentation help reduce bias.
   Collaboration with ethicists and domain experts is also valuable.

Q: What is TF-IDF?
A: TF-IDF stands for Term Frequency Inverse Document Frequency.
   It weighs words by how important they are across documents.
